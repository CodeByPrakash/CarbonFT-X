CarbonWise — Track, Visualize & Reduce Your Carbon Footprint

CarbonWise is a full-featured carbon footprint tracking and sustainability education web app. Log daily activities, see your impact in real time, and learn how to lower your footprint.

Track

Log activities across Transport, Food, Energy, and Shopping with real-time CO2 impact calculations and a live footprint summary.

Dashboard

An animated carbon score, footprint trend charts, weekly goal progress, a category breakdown, achievement badges, and personalized eco-tips.

Visualize

An interactive world map showing emission intensity by country, with global stats and side-by-side impact comparisons.

Learn

An interactive climate quiz, a "Day in Your Life" carbon simulator, and flip cards busting common climate myths.

Take Action

Filterable eco-action cards with difficulty and impact ratings, a personal commitments tracker, and a community impact counter.
